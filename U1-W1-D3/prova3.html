Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your dubs with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onios, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

FEATURED IN:

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups "(500gr)" all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 "(350gr)" lukewarm water

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes "(scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)"
  • 1 1/2cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.
